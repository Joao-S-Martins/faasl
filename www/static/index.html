Open Rehearsals

Band rehearsals have begun again in preperation for our anniversary celebration. Rehearsals are every Friday night, starting at 8PM. We're working on new pieces. Guests are welcome to sit-in and play or just listen. Check the Facebook link for up-to-date info and changes.

Spreading the Joy of Music

We are a non-profit group with over 30 years of experience performing and teaching music in the East San Francisco Bay. We have seen first hand the benefits music has provided our community. Please help us in our continued effort to spread of joy of music.
