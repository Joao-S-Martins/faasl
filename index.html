Filarmonica Artista Amadora

Spreading the Joy of Music

We are a non-profit group with over 30 years of experience performing and teaching music in the East San Francisco Bay. We have seen first hand the benefits music has provided our community. Please help us in our continued effort to spread of joy of music.
